📞 Emergency Contacts

Save these numbers — they can save your life

⚠️ Important: These are national helpline numbers for India. Save them in your phone now — not when you need them! In a real emergency, call immediately and give your location.

National Emergency Helplines

Available 24 hours, 7 days a week

🚨

Police Emergency

For immediate danger, theft, assault, or any crime

100
🏥

Ambulance

Medical emergencies requiring immediate attention

108
🔥

Fire Emergency

For fire accidents and rescue operations

101
📱

Universal Emergency Number

Works from any phone including mobile. Connects to police/fire/ambulance.

112

1091

Domestic Violence Helpline

Child Helpline

For children and girls in need of care and protection

1098

Cyber Crime Helpline

Report online harassment, morphed images, and cyber abuse

💻

Cyber Crime Helpline

For reporting online fraud, cyber stalking, morphed pictures

1930

📌 Pro Tip: Save at least 3 trusted contacts under a code name like "ICE" (In Case of Emergency) in your phone. Share your live location with them regularly.
