Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Q: Is the incident report form safe?
A: Yes. Reports are stored only on your device using browser localStorage. Nothing is sent to any server.

Q: Can I use SheShield anonymously?
A: Yes. The report form allows you to write "Anonymous" instead of your name.

Q: Is this project open source?
A: This is a college project created for educational purposes. You are welcome to learn from the code.
